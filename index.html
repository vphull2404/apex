Recovery & Focus

Breathing

4-4-4-4
Box Breathing
A stress-reduction technique used by military and athletes. Equal-timed inhale, hold, exhale, hold cycles calm the nervous system.
4-7-8
4-7-8 Breathing
Developed by Dr. Andrew Weil based on pranayama. Promotes sleep, reduces anxiety. Exhale is double the inhale for nervous system regulation.
Deep
Diaphragmatic Breathing
Belly breathing activates the diaphragm fully, increases oxygen delivery, and reduces blood pressure through parasympathetic activation.
Pranayama
Alternate Nostril
Nadi Shodhana pranayama. Balances the left and right hemispheres, calms the mind, and is used in yoga traditions to prepare for meditation.
2:4 Ratio
Pursed Lip Breathing
Recommended by respiratory therapists. Extends the exhale to slow breathing rate, improve ventilation, and reduce breathlessness during exercise.

Striking Fundamentals
👊
Jab
The foundational straight punch thrown with the lead hand. Used for range-finding, setting up combinations, and creating distance.
✊
Cross
A powerful straight punch from the rear hand. Rotates the hips and shoulders to generate maximum force through the kinetic chain.
🤜
Hook
A horizontal arc punch targeting the temple or jaw. Power comes from rotation of the core and pivot of the lead foot.
